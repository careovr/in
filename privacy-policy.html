Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 2024

Welcome to Careovr. We value your trust, and this Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information. It applies to all users, including Caregivers, Doctors, Blood Donors, Ambulance Owners, Users, and Hospitals. By using our website Careovr, you agree to the practices outlined below.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: We collect personal data such as name, email, phone number, location, and medical history (for users). Doctors, Caregivers, and Hospitals will also need to provide professional credentials.
  • Health Information: For users, we may collect health data like prescriptions, BMI, BMR, and appointment history.
  • Professional Information: Caregivers, Doctors, and Hospitals need to provide their licenses, qualifications, and registration details.
  • Payment Information: We collect payment information for transactions, memberships, and donations (for Blood Donors).
  • Device and Usage Data: Information such as IP address, device type, browser type, and interaction data is collected to improve our service.
  • Cookies: We use cookies to track user interactions, provide personalized experiences, and analyze behavior on the platform.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To Provide Services: We use your information to deliver our healthcare services such as connecting you with doctors, hospitals, caregivers, and blood donation matching.
  • For Marketing: With your consent, we may use your contact information to send promotional offers, health tips, and updates on new features or services.
  • Compliance with Laws: We may use your data to meet regulatory and legal requirements in emergency healthcare situations or when required by law.
  • Enhancing User Experience: Your usage data helps us improve our website's functionality and develop a more personalized experience for you.

3. Sharing Your Information

  • Healthcare Providers: We share relevant user data with hospitals, doctors, caregivers, and ambulance providers to provide healthcare services.
  • Blood Donor Information: In life-saving situations, donor data is shared with recipients with consent.
  • Third-Party Service Providers: We may work with trusted partners for payments, analytics, and customer service, who may access limited data.
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose your data to comply with legal requests, court orders, or other legal processes.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your data. However, no system is completely secure. We recommend safeguarding your account details and being cautious when using shared devices.

5. Your Rights

  • Access: You can request access to your personal data and healthcare history.
  • Update: You can correct any incorrect or outdated personal information by contacting us or using your profile dashboard.
  • Delete: You have the right to request that we delete your personal data, which may impact the services we can provide to you.
  • Opt-out: You may opt out of marketing communications at any time by unsubscribing from our emails or contacting us directly.

6. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If we become aware of this, we will delete the information.

7. International Users

If you are accessing Careovr from outside India, please be aware that your data may be transferred and stored in India. By using our services, you consent to this transfer.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be communicated through email or a prominent notice on our website. Your continued use of Careovr indicates acceptance of any changes.
